About FitFuel Pro

FitFuel Pro is an AI-powered nutrition assistant that helps you create healthy, personalized meal plans based on your goals, preferences, and available ingredients.

1

Choose Your Mode

Standard Mode: Select cuisine type, meal type, fitness goal, and macro targets for a fully customized meal plan.

Smart Pantry Mode: Input ingredients you have available, and AI will create recipes using what you already own.

2

Set Your Preferences

Select your fitness goal (Fat Loss, Muscle Building, Maintenance, or Endurance) and set macro targets (calories, protein, carbs, fiber). Leave blank for AI optimization.

3

Get AI-Generated Recipes

Our AI chef creates personalized recipes with:

  • Complete ingredient lists
  • Step-by-step instructions
  • Nutritional information
  • Cost breakdown per serving
  • Budget swap suggestions
  • YouTube cooking tutorials
4

Special Features

Cheat Meal Converter: Transform your cravings into healthy versions with smart ingredient substitutions.

Specific Recipe Generator: Get detailed recipes for any dish you want.

Cost Analysis: See ingredient costs and get budget-friendly swap suggestions.

Cooking Videos: Watch recipe tutorials directly in the app.

5

Export & Save

Copy recipes to clipboard or download as text file for offline use. Recipes include all nutritional information and cooking instructions.

Note: This app uses AI to generate recipes. Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized dietary advice.
